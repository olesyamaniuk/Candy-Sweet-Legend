About the Game

Candy Legend is a delightful match-3 puzzle game where players match colorful candies to complete fun objectives and progress through levels. Immerse yourself in a world full of sweetness, solve challenging puzzles, and collect rewards to unlock new levels and power-ups. With vibrant graphics and addictive gameplay, Candy Legend is the perfect game for puzzle lovers of all ages!

How to Play

  • Matching

    Match three or more candies of the same type to clear them and score points.

  • Strategy

    Strategize carefully to make the most of limited moves in each level.

  • Level Objectives

    Complete level objectives such as collecting specific candies or clearing obstacles.

  • Progress

    Unlock new levels and features as you progress through the game.

  • Boosters

    Use special candy boosters and power-ups to overcome challenging puzzles.

  • Events

    Participate in daily challenges and events for extra rewards and surprises.
